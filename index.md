Welcome to Middlebury College Geography major Sam Barnard's Spring 2026 700-level senior research project!

This began as an exploratory project with the primary goal of learning and applying spatial SQL with PostGIS. I wanted to work with NYC data partially because [NYC OpenData](https://opendata.cityofnewyork.us/) is such a great resource, and also because a lot of data on the scale of NYC is usually to big for analysis in desktop GIS. I chose to examine a question related to transportation out of personal interest and passion for the subject, as well as the fact that I could learn and apply the [pgRouting](https://pgrouting.org/) network analysis extension for PostGIS/PostgreSQL.
