This project was a very valuable learning experince, part of which involved recognizing the inefficiency of some of my methods. For the sake of the presentation of methods on this site, many extranious or exploratory queries were omitted to focus on my core workflow and analysis. Also worth noting is the fact that the analysis was done with later versions of PostGIS and its extentions, and therefore more efficient approaches may exist as well. 

While this project focused more on learning and applying GIS methods, a clear area for expansion is the integration of demographic or economic data such as from the ACS/Census, to explore possible racial or class disparities related to the spatial distribution of subway access.
