This project was a very valuable learning experince, part of which was recognizing the inefficiency of some of the methods. For the sake of the presentation of methods on this site, many extranious or exploratory queries were omitted to focus on the core workflow and analysis. Also worth noting is the fact that the analysis was done on later versions of PostGIS and its extentions, and therefore more efficient approaches may exist as well. While this project focused more on learning and applying GIS methods, a clear areas for expansion of this project is the integration of demographic or economic data such as from the ACS/Census, to explore possible ethnic or class disparities related to the spatial distribution of subway access.
